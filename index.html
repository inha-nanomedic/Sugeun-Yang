Overview

Prof. Su-Geun Yang directs the Integrated Research Lab for Medicinal Nano and Life Science at Inha University College of Medicine. His research spans nanoparticle-based drug delivery, polymer drug conjugates, photo-responsive therapy, and emerging space-medicine applications — with translational focus on cancer therapy, hepatocellular carcinoma, and the cellular response to space-relevant environmental stress.
